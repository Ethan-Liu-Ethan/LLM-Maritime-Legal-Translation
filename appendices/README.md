# Appendices

This folder contains the appendices referenced in the main paper. Due to journal word-length constraints, these appendices are hosted here rather than in the published article.

## File

| File | Description |
|------|-------------|
| [Appendices.pdf](Appendices.pdf) | Combined PDF containing Appendix 1, Appendix 2, and Appendix 3 |

## Contents

### Appendix 1 — International Maritime Conventions

A list of nine core international maritime conventions used as English-language source material for constructing the evaluation dataset. These include, for example, the International Convention for the Safety of Life at Sea (SOLAS), the United Nations Convention on the Carriage of Goods by Sea (Hamburg Rules), and others.

### Appendix 2 — Chinese Maritime Laws and Regulations

A list of nearly 50 Chinese maritime laws, administrative regulations, and departmental rules used as Chinese-language source material. These include, for example, the Maritime Traffic Safety Law of the People's Republic of China.

### Appendix 3 — Structured Translation Prompts

The structured prompts used to instruct each LLM during the translation evaluation. These prompts were designed based on the ReAct framework (Yao et al., 2023) and Chain-of-Thought principles (Wei et al., 2022), adapted for the specific challenges of legal translation (Cao, 2007; Alcaraz Varó & Hughes, 2014).
