An accident investigation institution shall, within 20 working days from the day when the investigation of the accident is completed, produce a Letter of Determination of a Vessel-Induced Pollution Accident and serve it on the parties concerned.
Where the ship has not been operated normally for 24 consecutive hours due to its failure to maintain the seaworthiness or the other conditions as agreed upon, the charterer shall not pay the hire for the operating time so lost, unless such failure was caused by the charterer.
Where a ship of Chinese nationality has any waterway traffic incident abroad, or is detained, prohibited from entering the port, prohibited from entering China, or is expelled out of a port (country) abroad, the shipping company shall report the relevant information to the maritime safety administration at the port of registry in a timely manner, and the maritime safety administration shall effectively conduct corresponding communication and coordination and provide necessary assistance.
A vessel and a pollution clean-up entity shall conclude a vessel-induced pollution clean-up agreement based on the sample of agreements issued by the national maritime safety administration.
Where a port operator or tally operator has committed any illegal act specified herein, such act shall be included in its credit record and be announced to the public.
“Relevant personnel of an enterprise” means the legal representative, principal person in charge, chief financial officer, person in charge of customs affairs, and other management personnel of the enterprise.
Where an operator of hazardous goods at ports is suspected of hiding hazardous goods among ordinary goods, or omitting the declaration of hazardous goods or falsely declaring hazardous goods as ordinary ones, the port administrative department or maritime safety administration in the place where it is located may open the goods for examination and inspection according to the law, and the operator of hazardous goods at ports shall be cooperative.
Auction of the cargo carried by a ship shall be conducted by an auction organization composed of execution officers appointed, and auctioneers engaged by the maritime court, or conducted by an agency authorized by the maritime court.
The “vessels of foreign nationality purchased or chartered under the conditions of bareboat charter” as mentioned in these Provisions shall include the vessels of foreign nationality that have been purchased from abroad or chartered under the conditions of bareboat charter but for which no lawful vessel inspection certificate or certificate of nationality has not been obtained in China, as well as the vessels of foreign nationality that are purchased by way of auction.
If the Ministry of Communications approves the planning, or its suggestion on amending the planning is adopted, or it fails to put forward opinion within the above-mentioned time limit, the people's government of the related province, autonomous region, or municipality directly under the Central Government may publish and bring into effect the planning.
When the ship survey organization of Chinese-flagged ships and/or waterborne installations is changed, the new survey organization shall retrieve the existing survey certificate for file and issue new certificate.
For any operator's conduct in violation of the laws and regulations that is found in supervision and inspection or is subject to complaint and tip-off, information about the handling of such conduct and information about maritime accidents, the departments responsible for water transport management shall make records in the integrity files.
When berthing or operating at sea, an offshore installation shall have legal certificates and documents, and be manned with personnel with required professional skills, such as skills in collision avoidance, signal, communication, firefighting and life-saving.
Where a port operator, a port tally business operator or an operator engaging in port services for vessels or the rental and maintenance of port facilities, equipment and machinery commits any violation of these Provisions, the port administrative department shall, in accordance with the provisions of relevant laws and administrative regulations, include its credit information in the waterway transportation credit information management system and publish such information.
If an employer who has entered into a labor contract with a seafarer purchases commercial insurance for the seafarer, it shall not be discharged thereby from its statutory obligation to purchase work-related injury insurance for the seafarer. 
The right of mortgage with respect to a ship is the right of preferred compensation enjoyed by the mortgagee of that ship from the proceeds of the auction sale made in accordance with law where and when the mortgagor fails to pay his debt to the mortgagee secured by the mortgage of that ship.
Where an examination and approval authority for the use of port coastlines and any of its staff members abuses his or her power, neglects his or her duties, practices favoritism, or makes falsification, the relevant administrative department shall impose an administrative sanction.
The plans of tenders and auctions shall be formulated by the maritime administrative departments and shall be submitted to the competent people's government for approval and implementation.
The waterway maintenance administrative department shall, according to its duties, strengthen the supervision and administration of waterway maintenance through key inspection or random inspection.
The application shall clearly state the particulars of the maritime claim, the reasons for the application, the subject matter of the security and the amount of security required, with relevant evidence attached.
Where a port operator for hazardous goods violating the provisions of the Work Safety Law of the People's Republic of China is ordered to take corrective action, and is subject to a fine, but refuses to take corrective action, the port administrative department may, from the day after it is ordered to take corrective action, impose continuous penalties upon it on a daily basis according to the original amount.
An operator of hazardous goods at ports that fails to withdraw or use work safety expenses according to the law, thus failing to meet the work safety conditions shall be ordered by the port administrative department in the place where the operator is located to take corrective action within a prescribed time limit.
Where the insured amount is lower than the value for contribution under the general average, the insurer shall be liable for the general average contribution in the proportion that the insured amount bears to the value for contribution.
To apply for engaging in water transport business or modify the scope of water transport business, an applicant shall submit the application form to the department responsible for water transport management under the local people's government at the districted city level, together with the relevant materials proving that the applicant meets the requirements of these Provisions.
If any foreigner or foreign vessel causes pollution to a fishing port or waters of a fishing port of the People's Republic of China, the regulatory authority of fishery administration and fishing ports of the People's Republic of China may, according to the circumstances and the degree of damage, give a warning or impose a fine of not more than 100,000 yuan.
Where the international shipping operators engaged in international liner shipping make any liner conference agreement, operation agreement, or freight rate agreement, etc. between themselves, they shall submit the copy of the agreement to the department in charge of transportation under the State Council for record within 15 days from the day on which the agreement is made.
Where the parties to a contract of carriage agree that the consignee may directly request the carrier to deliver the goods, while the carrier fails to deliver the goods to the consignee or deliver the goods by contract, and the consignee requests the carrier to make a compensation, the people's court shall accept the request.
With respect to an issuing authority that does not perform its duties to administer the application for and issuance and use of the Seafarer's Passport in accordance with the law, the Maritime Safety Administration of the People's Republic of China may cancel the relevant designation.
A shipping company shall, within 3 months prior to the anniversary of the compliance certification, apply for an annual examination, the vessel shall, within the period between the second anniversary and the third anniversary of the safety management certificate, apply for an interim examination.
